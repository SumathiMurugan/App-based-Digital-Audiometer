Check your hearing

Who the test is for

The online hearing test is for people of all ages who have not been diagnosed with hearing loss.

How it works

The test measures how well you can hear beep sounds at certain frequencies.

What you’ll get

You’ll receive information about your hearing and a hearing test document. You can also consult a doctor if needed.
